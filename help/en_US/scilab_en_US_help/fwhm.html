fwhm

This function computes peak full width at half minimum or at another level of peak minimum for vector or matrix data y supplied as input.

Calling Sequence

f = fwhm (y)
f = fwhm (x, y)
f = fwhm (…, "zero")
f = fwhm (…, "min")
f = fwhm (…, "alevel", level)
f = fwhm (…, "rlevel", level)

Parameters

y:

vector or matrix
