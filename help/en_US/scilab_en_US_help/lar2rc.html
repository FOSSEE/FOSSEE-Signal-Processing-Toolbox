lar2rc

Calling Sequence

k = lar2rc(g)

Parameters

g:

define log area ratios.

k:

returns the reflection coefficients.

Examples

X = [7 6 5 8 3 6 8 7 5 2 4 7 4 3 2 5 4 9 5 3 5 7 3 9 4 1 2 0 5 4 8 6 4 6 5 3];
k = lar2rc(X)
or t=[2 5 6; 8 6 5; 8 9 4]
k = lar2rc(t)
